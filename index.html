Certification Levels

0

1

Site validated via NGO, community, or node. Documented progress on key gaps.
5
Certified with Distinction
Full LCA, verified closure, traceable product. Certified closure + audit passed.
6
LCA Prestige
Carbon-neutral or better, verified LCA. Climate leadership achieved.

EMC Minerals and Metals Miner's Pledge

As miners, we recognize the urgency of addressing climate risk and reducing emissions. Our commitment to achieving net-zero emissions by 2050, with interim targets set for 2030 and 2040, is essential in managing our climate impact now and for future generations.

We acknowledge that addressing a critical challenge like global carbon emissions demands collaboration, innovation, and stakeholder commitment. Our defined actions focus on Origin Traceability Assurances, Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Criteria, Climate and Carbon Protocols, and Reclamation and Closure Practices, guiding our meaningful progress toward a sustainable future.
